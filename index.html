About Me

My name is Jeff, and I'm a full-stack web developer from Grand Prairie, Texas. My skills include HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Materialize, Javascript, jQuery, Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, and React. I enjoy being able to learn something new about software development every day. In the near future, I plan on adding Python and Angular to my developer skills, as well as improving upon my current skills.

I have a wonderful wife and three amazing kids with another on the way. Being able to spend more time with them is what drives me to learn web development and finally get away from my current career as a truck driver.

In my free time, I spend most of it watching videos and learning something new about coding. But when I'm not coding, I love bowling, playing basketball, cooking, and finding new places to eat.
